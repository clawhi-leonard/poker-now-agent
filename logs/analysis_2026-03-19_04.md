## Critical Bug: Concurrent Rebuy Chaos (04:02:42)

### What happened
At 04:02:42, THREE bots simultaneously hit the <1500 stack threshold:
- AceBot: Low stack (798) — proactive rebuy  
- NitKing: Low stack (798) — proactive rebuy
- Clawhi: Low stack (788) — proactive rebuy

This caused:
1. AceBot left seat, reloaded page, submitted seat request
2. NitKing left seat, reloaded, found itself "seated with 101 chips", got stuck in leave-resit loop
3. Clawhi (HOST) also left seat to rebuy — **no one could approve AceBot or NitKing's requests**
4. AceBot got falsely detected as busted (last_known=15722 — not actually busted!)
5. CallStn also got falsely busted (last_known=1)

### Root Cause
- No serialization of rebuys — the `rebuy_lock` exists but isn't preventing concurrent rebuy *decisions*
- Host should rebuy LAST so it can approve others first
- When a bot leaves seat for rebuy, other bots see it as "not seated" → false bust trigger
- NitKing "seated but stack=101" loop: pokernow preserves the bot's current stack on reload, so re-sitting gives the same crumb stack, not 5000

### Impact
- 3+ minutes of no poker while bots sort out rebuys
- False bust detections, wasted rebuy attempts
- NitKing stuck in infinite leave-resit loop
