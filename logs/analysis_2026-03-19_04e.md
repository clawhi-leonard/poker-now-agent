## Remaining Issue: "Seated with Crumbs"

This is the #1 remaining problem. When a bot with a crumb stack (1-42 chips) reloads pokernow:
1. Pokernow auto-seats the bot with its previous crumb stack
2. Bot sees "stack=3, < 1500" → tries to leave and resit
3. Leave → reload → auto-seats again with crumbs → loop
4. After 3 loops, v17 gives up and falls through to bust path
5. Bust path eventually works, but wastes ~60-90 seconds per rebuy

### Possible Solutions
1. **Don't leave seat before rebuy** — find "Add Chips" or "Top Up" in Options menu
2. **Click a DIFFERENT seat** after leaving (avoid the one pokernow remembers)
3. **Wait longer after leaving** before reloading (let pokernow clear the association)
4. **Use a cookie/localStorage flag** to tell pokernow we want a fresh seat
