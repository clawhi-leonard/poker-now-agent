### 2. Raise Cap Bypass on River Free Checks ✅ CRITICAL FIX
- v17 had a bug: `raise_capped` killed `can_raise` in big pots (>1500 = 30% of buy-in)
- This meant bots couldn't value bet rivers even with 99% equity if the pot was large
- **v18 fix:** River free checks use original `can_raise` (from actions_str), not the capped version
- The raise cap correctly prevents re-raise wars but no longer blocks opening value bets

### 3. LAG Fold Rate Fixed ✅
